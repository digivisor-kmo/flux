How to play

Classic · mode 1 of 2

One rule. Tap a tile: that tile and its neighbours (up, down, left, right) shift one color forward. Bring the whole board to a single color.

Symbols. Hard to tell the colors apart? Tap the symbols button top right and every color also gets its own shape (triangle, circle, square).

Numbers · mode 2 of 2

Hidden wiring. Each numbered tile secretly flips two other tiles between green and red — the tapped tile itself doesn't change. Tap around to learn which tiles are linked, then turn every tile green.

The eye button top right lets you peek at each tile's hidden wiring.
